EXPLORE THE RELATIONSHIP BETWEEN TWO VARIABLES

Lets make a simple scatterplot with absences on the horizontal axis (x-axis), and math scores on the vertical axis (y-axis), with labels.

We now estimate a regression (looking at the relationship between variables). Lets add a regression line based on the linear regression model, in order to look at the effect of the IV (Independent variable) on the DV (Dependent Variable).

model <- lm(schooldata$math_score ~ schooldata$absences,
            data = schooldata)
plot(schooldata$absences, schooldata$math_score, 
     xlab = "Absences",
     ylab = "Math Scores", 
     main = "Math Scores based on Number of Absences")
abline(model, col="red") 

# What type of relationship exists between math scores and attendance?

summary(model)
